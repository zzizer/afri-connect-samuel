AfriConnect?

AfriConnect is an online free plattform that gives companies and individuals, an opportunity to interact together in terms of Employment. It was designed to make employment better, fair, easy and Time Saving.

Companies can easily add employment opportunities, schedule interviews virtually for the candidates and do meet thier specifications. If a company add an job opportunities, and individuals manage applying, the company has access to view, ealuate and monitor the academic transcripts of those individuals.

Individuals can can view available job opportunities across the Globe, save and monitor their academic transcripts as well. An individual, after completion of the requirements, can automatically generate a Cirriculum Vitae, application Letters, Resume and so many Others.
